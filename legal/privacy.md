## How MakerQ is different (read this first)

MakerQ is **self-hosted**. The application runs entirely on your own computer. The quote requests,
customer details, uploaded files, and business settings you enter into MakerQ are stored **locally on
your machine** and are **never transmitted to, collected by, or stored by us.** We cannot see your
business data or your customers' data. For that data, **you** are the controller and are responsible
for its security, backups, and legal compliance.

## 1. Information we collect

- **Account & license:** your name and email address, used to issue and validate your license and to
  provide support.
- **Payments:** when you buy Pro or Lifetime, payment is processed by **Stripe**. We do **not** receive
  or store your full card number. We receive limited transaction data (e.g., email, plan, payment
  status, card brand/last4) needed to grant your license.
- **Activation keys:** we generate and email you an activation key, and store your email and that key
  so your purchase can be validated.
- **Crash reports (optional):** if the app crashes, it emails a diagnostic log **only if you have
  entered a crash-report email in Settings → About → Diagnostics.** By default nothing is sent. Crash
  logs contain technical diagnostics plus your business/licensee name and email for support context;
  they do **not** contain your customers' data.
- **Website:** makerq.io may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics and cookies. [Confirm/adjust with
  your actual site setup.]
- **Support:** if you email us, we retain those messages.

## 2. How we use your information

- Provide and validate your license; deliver activation keys.
- Process payments and prevent fraud (via Stripe).
- Provide support and send transactional/service messages (e.g., your key email, receipts).
- Diagnose crashes you choose to report and improve the product.

**We do not sell your personal information.**

## 3. Service providers (processors)

- **Stripe** — payment processing.
- **Supabase** — stores your license/entitlement record (email, activation key, plan, status).
- **Resend** — sends transactional email (activation keys, notices) from noreply@makerq.io.
- **GitHub** — hosts application downloads and updates.

Each processes data only as needed to provide its service.

## 4. Data retention

We keep your license/account data while your license is active and as needed for legal, tax, and
accounting purposes, then delete or anonymize it.

process about **your own** customers stays under your control on your machine, not ours.
