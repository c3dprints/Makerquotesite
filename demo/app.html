Privacy & Disclaimer

What this app records: MakerQ runs entirely on your own computer. Your quote requests, customer details, uploaded files, and settings are stored locally on this machine only.

What C3D Prints receives: Only your business (licensee) name and email address, used to issue and validate your license, plus any feedback you choose to send. No customer information is ever transmitted to, recorded by, or stored at c3dprints.com.

Your responsibility: As the operator of this self-hosted app, you are solely responsible for the customer information you collect, store, and process - including its security, backups, and compliance with applicable privacy laws.
