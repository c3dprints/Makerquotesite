Outgoing email (send quotes)

Quotes send from noreply@makerq.io. Customers see a note that the inbox isn't monitored and to reply to you, so set your notification email on the Account tab — replies go there. Uncheck the box above to use your own mailbox instead.

Send from your own mailbox (SMTP). Use an app password where supported, not your main password.

⚠ If your mailbox has 2-factor authentication (2FA) on, SMTP may not work — many providers (including GoDaddy) block password logins with 2FA and don’t offer app passwords for SMTP. Use MakerQ hosted email, or turn off 2FA on that mailbox. Also note: you can’t log in with an alias — use the real mailbox as the Username and put the alias in From address.

What C3D Prints receives: Only your business (licensee) name and email address, used to issue and validate your license, plus any feedback you choose to send. No customer information is ever transmitted to, recorded by, or stored at c3dprints.com.
